Maps

There are currently 7 active maps for tournament games:

  • Dust 2
  • Mirage
  • Inferno
  • Overpass
  • Nuke
  • Train
  • Vertigo

There are also non-tournament maps available for play:

  • Chlorine
  • Anubis
  • Office
  • Cbblestone

Pistol

CT Default pistol is USP-S/P2000 USP-S has few bullets but silent. P2000 has more bullets but higher recoil on long range.

T always use 9mm GLOCK as their default pistol. It has the weakest damage but many bullets, only second to five-seven.

Purchasable pistol:

  • Default Pistol B-1-1
  • Dual-Beretta B-1-2
  • P250 B-1-3
  • Five-Seven/Tec9/CZ75 B-1-4
  • Desert Eagle/Revolver B-1-5

Heavy Weapons

Consist of shotgun & Machine guns, high damage but minimal range

Purchasable weapons:

  • Nova B-2-1
  • Auto Shotgun B-2-2
  • Mag7/Sawed Off B-2-3
  • Negev B-2-4
  • M249 B-2-5
